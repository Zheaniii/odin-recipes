Creamy Potato and Leek Soup

Description

There is nothing better on a chilly, damp day than a steaming bowl of potato leek soup.
This creamy potato leek soup pairs perfectly with sourdough bread for a comforting meal, and tastes even better heated up the next day.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup butter
  • 2 Leeks, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon, cornstarch
  • 1 Quart, chicken stock
  • 1 Pinch, Cayenne pepper
  • 4 Cups, Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 Cups heavy cream, or to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in soup pot over medium-high heat. Add leeks and salt; cook until leeks begin to wilt. Reduce heat to medium or medium-low and cook until soft and almost pastelike, 30 to 40 minutes.
  2. Place potatoes in the pot; add chicken broth. Adjust heat so mixture simmers slowly. Cook until potatoes are soft, about 30 minutes. Puree mixture with an immersion blender or in batches in a blender. Whisk in heavy cream. Heat over medium heat. Add more salt, if needed, and cayenne pepper. Do not boil.
  3. Serve with a drizzle of heavy cream and a sprinkle of chopped chives.
